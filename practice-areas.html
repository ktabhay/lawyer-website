Focus Areas

Areas of Legal Practice

Estate Planning & Succession

Individuals and families on estate planning, succession strategies, inheritance structuring, and wealth preservation. Services include drafting and execution of wills, obtaining probate and succession certificates, family settlement arrangements, transfer and transmission of immovable and movable assets, transmission of shares and securities, and advisory on stamp duty and registration implications.

Trusts

Establishment of Trust (Private & Charitable), structuring, and administration of private family trusts, discretionary trusts, and charitable trusts. Advises on trust governance, trustee responsibilities, regulatory compliance, and succession planning through trust structures.

Real Estate Transactions

Represents corporate entities, investors, developers, and individual clients in the acquisition, sale, transfer, leasing, and development of agricultural, residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Provides comprehensive legal support throughout the transaction lifecycle.

Land Due Diligence & Title Search

Conducts detailed title verification, ownership investigations, encumbrance checks, and legal due diligence of land and property assets. Identifies potential legal risks and advises clients on risk mitigation before acquisition or investment.

Property Documentation

Drafting and vetting of various legal documents viz. Lease Deeds, LOI, MoU, Gift Deeds, Conveyance Deed, Sale Deed, Relinquishment Deeds, Rectification Deeds, Wills, Trust Deed, Memorandums of Understandings, Memorandum of Family Settlement, Deed of Declarations, General/Special Power of Attorneys, various agreements including Equipment Leasing Agreement, Leave and License Agreements, Agreement to Sell, Collaboration Agreements, Addendum, Service Agreements, Franchise Agreements, employment contracts, etc.

Mutation & Property Records

Mutation and updating of ownership records before statutory and municipal authorities, including DDA, MCD, NDMC, revenue authorities, and other governmental bodies.

Freehold Conversion

Conversion of leasehold properties into freehold properties before the Delhi Development Authority (DDA), Land & Development Office (L&DO), and other competent authorities, including preparation and submission of requisite documentation.

RERA Compliance

Provides legal and regulatory advisory under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA), including project registration, compliance obligations, documentation requirements, dispute resolution, and regulatory representation.

Corporate & Regulatory Compliance

Advises companies, promoters, and business entities on corporate governance, legal compliance, regulatory obligations, corporate due diligence, commercial transactions, and day-to-day legal and strategic matters, Insolvency and legal compliance.

Consumer Law

Represents and advises consumers, businesses, and service providers in consumer disputes, unfair trade practice matters, product liability claims, and proceedings before Consumer Commissions and regulatory authorities.

Intellectual Property

Provides trademark search, registration, prosecution, assignment, licensing, and enforcement services. Advises businesses on brand protection strategies and intellectual property management.

Taxation

Advises individuals, businesses, and family offices on income tax matters, tax-efficient structuring of transactions, capital gains implications, and tax considerations arising from property and succession-related transactions.Handling cases pertaining to Income Tax before the appropriate forums, including Appellate Jurisdiction, involving personal appearances before various departments of the Income Tax Department, CIT (A) and Income Tax Tribunal with respect

Insurance & Banking

Legal support in banking documentation, loan transactions, mortgage agreements, insurance disputes, recovery proceedings and financial regulatory matters.

Family & Matrimonial Disputes

Representation in divorce, child custody, domestic violence, maintenance, guardianship, family settlements and matrimonial dispute resolution matters.

Litigation Management

Managing civil, commercial, succession, consumer, property and regulatory disputes before courts, tribunals, arbitral forums and quasi-judicial authorities.

Legal Drafting & Vetting

Drafting, reviewing and negotiating contracts, deeds, agreements, notices, legal opinions, policies and documentation protecting legal and commercial interests.
