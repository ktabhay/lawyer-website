Areas of Legal Practice

Civil Litigation

Comprehensive support across civil disputes — from contract disagreements and recovery suits to injunctions and appeals. We prepare every filing carefully and represent clients firmly before civil courts at each stage of the proceeding.

Property Law

Expert advice on sale deeds, title verification, tenancy disputes, partition suits and property-related litigation. We help clients secure their property rights and resolve disputes through negotiation or court proceedings.

Criminal Matters

Strong defense and guidance through criminal proceedings, including bail applications, FIR quashing, trial representation and appeals — always with discretion and a clear strategy for the client's defense.

Corporate Advisory

Legal advisory and compliance support for businesses — drafting and reviewing agreements, handling regulatory filings, and advising on day-to-day legal risk so companies can operate with confidence.
